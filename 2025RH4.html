Asteroid 2025 RH4

Asteroid 2025 RH4 is a near-Earth object that made a distant close approach on September 29, 2025, passing at a distance of approaximately 17.8 lunar distances, or about 6.8 million kilometers from our planet. Measuring roughly 63 meters in diameter, it was traveling at a speed of about 17km/s. Had it impacted Earth, the asteroid would have released an energy equivalent to approximately 14 megatons of TNT, similar to the energy released by the largest nuclear weapons. While this event did not pose a threat, it underscores the importance of planetary defense monitoring.

Key Facts

  • Size: Approximately 63 meters (207 feet) in diameter.
  • Speed: Traveling at about 17 km/s(61,200 km/h).
  • Impact Energy (Hypothetical): Equivalent to approximately 14 megatons of TNT.
  • Closest Approach Distance: About 6.8 million kilometers from Earth.
