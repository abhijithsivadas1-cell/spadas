Asteroid 2025 SX10

Asteroid 2025 SX10 is a near-Earth object that also made a close approach to Earth on September 30, 2025. With a diameter of about 54 feet,it traveled at a speed of about 18,073 miles per hour(29,080 km/h). The asteroid passed at a distance of 1.58 million miles (2.54 million kilometers) from earth, which is approximately 6.6 times the average distance to the moon, and it was confirmed to pose **no threat** to Earth.This event underscores the importance of continuous monitoring of near earth objects to assess potential risks and develop mitigation strategies.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: Approximately 16.5 meters (54 feet).
  • Speed: Around 18,073 mph (29,080 km/h).
  • Closest Approach Distance: 1.58 million miles (2.54 million km) from Earth ( 6.6 lunar distances).
  • Date of Close Approach: September 30, 2025.
