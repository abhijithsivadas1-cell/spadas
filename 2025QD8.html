Asteroid 2025 QD8

Asteroid 2025 QD8 is a newly identified near-Earth object belonging to the **Apollo group** of asteroids, which are characterized by their elongated orbits that cross Earth’s path around the Sun. It is relatively small in cosmic terms,estimated to measure only a few tens of meters across, yet its size is large enough to cause regional damage if it were ever to collide with Earth. This asteroid travels at high velocity ,completing its orbit in just over a year,and its trajectory brings it into Earth's neighborhood during close approaches, and its composition is believed to be rocky material mixed with metal content.

Key Facts

  • Size: Estimated between 17 and 38 meters (55 to 124 feet) in diameter (roughly the size of a commercial jetliner or a large bus).
  • Closest Approach: Passed within approximately 135,500 miles (218,000 kilometers) of Earth, about 57% of the average distance to the Moon.
  • Speed: Traveling at speeds exceeding 45,000 km/h.
  • Orbit: Belongs to the Apollo group of asteroids, which have orbits that cross Earth's path.
