Asteroid 2025 SA3

Asteroid 2025 SA3 is a near-Earth object that made a close approach to Earth on September 30, 2025. Measuring approximately 16.5 meters in diameter about the size of a house,it traveled at a speed of 18,073 miles per hour. It passed at a distance of 1.67 million miles from earth, which is approximately seven times the average distance to the Moon. NASA confirmed that 2025 SA3 posed **no threat** to our planet.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: Approximately 16.5 meters (54 feet).
  • Speed: About $18,073\text{ mph}$ ($29,080\text{ km/h}$).
  • Closest Approach Distance: $1.67\text{ million miles}$ ($2.69\text{ million km}$) from Earth ($\sim 7\text{ lunar distances}$).
  • Date of Close Approach: September 30, 2025.
